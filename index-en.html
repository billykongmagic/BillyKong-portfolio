Artist Profile

Illusionist · Creative Producer · Stage Director

Handpicked by world-renowned illusionist Franz Harary as a core member of his Asia touring team, Billy Kong brings two decades of international stage experience to every performance — and more than 3,000 shows of proven, repeatable excellence.

His touring footprint spans Hong Kong, Shenzhen, and Hainan — Billy is one of the few Macau-based artists with both an international touring team background and large-scale theatre production credentials.

With residencies at Macau's finest five-star hotels and brand collaborations including Hermès, Billy understands the expectations of a luxury audience — and consistently exceeds them.
